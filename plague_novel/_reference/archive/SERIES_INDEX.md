## SERIES OVERVIEW

The Genesis Protocol is a 13-book series following a 3,200-year genetic conspiracy across human history. Two competing networks—one defensive (preventing collapse), one offensive (controlling who survives)—battle through civilization's greatest upheavals.

**Core Concept**: A genetic breeding program encoding survival knowledge in human DNA, passed through bloodlines from the Bronze Age Collapse to the modern era.

**Narrative Structure**:
- Books alternate between historical eras and modern timeline
- Each book reveals one of seven "keys" to understanding the Protocol
- Modern timeline (Book 2) brings all threads together
- Historical books build evidence the modern protagonist uncovers
