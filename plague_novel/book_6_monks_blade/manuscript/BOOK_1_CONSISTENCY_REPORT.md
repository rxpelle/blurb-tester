## 2. WHO GIVES THOMAS THE IRON EYE? ⚠️ CRITICAL

**Book 1 Chapter 1:**
- Brother Hamo is killed by Gray Robes while copying a Boethius commentary
- Hamo presses the Iron Eye into Thomas's hand before dying
- "Brother Hamo pressed something into my palm. Cold iron. The key."

**Book 6 Chapter 8:**
- Wilhelm passes the Iron Eye directly to Thomas in January 1347
- "He pressed the Iron Eye into Thomas's hand"
- No mention of Hamo as intermediary

**CONTRADICTION:**
Book 1 shows Hamo physically giving Thomas the key. Book 6 shows Wilhelm giving it directly. Both cannot be true.

**RECOMMENDED FIX:**
Revise Book 6 Chapter 8 to show:
1. Wilhelm gives the Iron Eye to Hamo with instructions: "If anything happens to me, give this to Thomas of Eltville. Tell him what I discovered."
2. Wilhelm dies from plague shortly after
3. Hamo holds the key for a few days/weeks
4. Gray Robes attack, Hamo is dying, he passes key to Thomas (matching Book 1)

**Impact:** MEDIUM - Requires adding 2-3 paragraphs to Book 6 Chapter 8

## 4. WILHELM'S DEATH TIMING CONFUSION ⚠️ CRITICAL

**Book 6 Chapter 8:**
- Wilhelm born: 1310 CE
- Age 36 in 1346 CE (meeting Thomas)
- Dies: **January 1347 CE** at age 37

**Book 1 Chapter 1:**
- Thomas remembers: "Wilhelm had died when I was eight"
- But also: "One month ago. Wilhelm's deathbed." (from 1347 perspective)
- These contradict each other

**Book 1 Chapter 2:**
- "One month ago. Wilhelm's deathbed."
- Places death in late 1346 or early 1347

**CONTRADICTION:**
"When I was eight" would mean Wilhelm died around 1329 CE (if Thomas born 1321). But Chapter 2 and Book 6 both show Wilhelm dying January 1347.

**EXPLANATION:**
The "when I was eight" memory is Thomas recalling when Wilhelm FIRST SHOWED HIM MANUSCRIPTS, not when Wilhelm died. The writing is ambiguous.

**RECOMMENDED FIX:**
Revise Book 1 Chapter 1 to clarify the memory:

**Current text:**
"I was eight years old, standing in his father's vineyard... Wilhelm had died when I was eight."

**Revised text:**
"I was eight years old when Wilhelm first showed me the manuscripts, standing in my father's vineyard... That memory feels like another life now. One month ago, Wilhelm lay dying from plague."

**Impact:** LOW - Simple clarification, one paragraph revision
