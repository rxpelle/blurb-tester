# Quintus Publius Scipio

Carthaginian rhetor and defensive carrier; the 4th-century descendant of [Marcus Publius](marcus_publius.md) who carries Pattern Eye between Helena and Monnica. Named and written on-page in Book 5 chapter 1 (2026-04-24 rewrite).

- **Bloodline:** Gen 51 (same cohort as [Jerome](jerome.md); one generation senior to [Augustine](augustine_of_hippo.md) Gen 52)
- **Dates:** ~275 – ~352 CE (age 50 at Nicaea 325, age 77 at his death in Carthage 352)
- **Profession:** Rhetor in Carthage; nominal Christian observer at the Council of Nicaea (325)
- **First appears:** Book 5, chapter 1
- **Raw sources:** `../../book_5_augustine_protocol/manuscript/chapters/chapter_01_constantine_era.md`

## Role in Book 5

- **Nicaea, June 325 CE** — Attends Council as a junior observer; his POV opens chapter 1. Does not yet hold Pattern Eye; is in Constantine's orbit only as a literate provincial.
- **Jerusalem, Spring 327 CE** — Receives Pattern Eye from [Helena](helena_mother_of_constantine.md) at the Golgotha excavation. Helena is frail, age ~77; Quintus is 52. She chooses him because his family line (descent from Marcus Publius) was flagged to her by an Alexandrian carrier during her earlier Eastern travels.
- **Carthage, Summer 352 CE** — Dying of respiratory illness at age 77, Quintus transfers Pattern Eye to [Monnica](monica.md), who is ~21 and newly married. He handles the Gen 53 mother / Gen 52 son paradox diegetically: *"There are people who count these things and there are people who carry them. I am the carrier. You will be the carrier after me. The counting is for other people."*
- **Tagaste, November 13, 354 CE** — Referenced only: Monnica holds the disc in her hand while nursing her newborn son Aurelius Augustinus. Quintus is two years dead; his work is complete.
