- **Generation:** **Gen 52 (canonical, resolved 2026-04-23).** Supersedes master_timeline's Gen 54 and outline's "17 generations from Marcus Publius" (both errors). Source of truth: `BOOK_BASELINE.md`.

### Genetic memory activation (chapters 1–2)

The 386 CE Milan garden episode is rendered as both authentic spiritual conversion *and* defensive network mission activation. Memories arrive with *knowledge*: details, names, languages he never learned (4th-century-BCE Greek dialect, exact phrases from Origen's *De Principiis*). Monica, who has carried the Pattern Eye since receiving it from Marcus Publius's line in the ~360s CE, recognizes the activation pattern and begins his training. Within the year she dies in Ostia and physically transfers the key.

### The encoding strategy (chapters 5, 6, 8, 9)

Augustine cannot teach defensive mode openly — the offensive network controls Church doctrine through [Jerome](jerome.md) (Vulgate translator) and allied bishops. He hides the methodology inside acceptable theological language. Both layers are genuine: the surface satisfies the Church, the depth instructs future carriers.

### Major works as defensive instruments

**Confessions (~397–400, 13 books):** Spiritual autobiography. Books 1–9 are surface-readable conversion narrative; Book 10 introduces memory as theological category and is the methodological core; Books 11–13 (on time, Genesis) embed the cosmological + bloodline framework. Template for *nous* meditation transmission.

**City of God (413–426, 22 books):** Magnum opus. Beyond its theological content, **Books XV–XXI deliberately encode coordinates for the seven keys** as identified by [Atticus](atticus.md) and the Constantinople network:

| Book | Encoded artifact | Key location |
|------|------------------|--------------|
| XV | Pattern Eye | Hippo basilica reliquary, third-course foundation stones, eastern wall |
| XVI | Memory Stone + Bronze Mirror | Mount of Olives baptistery foundation (Stone) + Sketis monastery, Mary's Chapel (Mirror) |
| XVII | Speaking Scroll | Hagia Sophia northern crypt, sealed vault |
| XVIII | Dream Tablet | Lérins Island scriptorium foundation, eastern corner |
| XIX–XX | Time Chain (search pattern) | Apostolic sites, Roman crypts, North African churches |
| XXI | Truth Seal (search pattern) | Locations encoded but uncertain — Truth Seal possibly lost in Rome's collapse |

This is **the structural reason *City of God* must survive the Dark Ages**: it doubles as a treasure map for future carriers (Maria of Toledo's medieval network, Sarah Chen's modern decoding).

**Anti-Pelagian treatises (412–428):** *On the Spirit and the Letter*, *On Nature and Grace*, *On the Predestination of the Saints*, etc. Surface: refutation of Pelagius's heresy. Encoded: detailed pattern instructions on genetic memory transmission disguised as grace doctrine. Augustine condemns Pelagius publicly to maintain cover — the two are aligned defensively.

**Letter 130 to Proba (412 CE), letters to Paulinus, etc.:** Pastoral correspondence functioning as network coordination.

**Vita Augustini by [Possidius](possidius.md) (post-430 CE):** Not by Augustine but the manuscript treats it as part of the encoded oeuvre — Possidius's biography is also a network handover document.

- Augustine's texts dominate Western Christianity for 1,000+ years — the encoded methodology survives as monastic practice (contemplation, pattern recognition, community life). Practitioners of Hesychasm (East), Benedictine and Cistercian orders (West) inherit defensive practice without knowing its origin.
