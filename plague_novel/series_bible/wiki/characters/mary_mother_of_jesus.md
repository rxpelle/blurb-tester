## Continuity notes

- Jesus's half-siblings become Branch 2 — diluted by Gen 45 (~100 CE), lost track by 200 CE. If any surface in Book 4 as secondary characters, they're defensive-weak.
- Joseph's Davidic-line ancestry is from a *separate* branch — he is not a Tausret-line carrier. Jesus's carrier status comes entirely through Mary.
- Gen 41 is a narratively loaded number — Essene prophetic tradition anticipates the 40th-gen carrier (off by one, since Jesus arrives in Gen 42). Raw calls this "off by one in ancient counting" — suggests Book 4 can play with the near-miss.
