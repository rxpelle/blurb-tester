# Marcus Publius

Roman census official; defensive carrier; the Book 4 → Book 5 bridge character. Witnesses Jesus's crucifixion (32 CE). Infiltrates the offensive network in Alexandria. **1st-century CE figure only** — resolved 2026-04-23 (see below).

- **Bloodline:** Gen 51 (per Book 5 chapters). Ancestor of [Monica](monica.md) and [Augustine](augustine_of_hippo.md) across ~11 generations (~300 years).
- **Dates:** ~5–~80 CE (rough bracket — historical census officials at the crucifixion would have been adults ~30 CE). **Not alive at 410 CE Rome's sack.** The figure at the sack is a different descendant of his line (see [continuity notes](#continuity-notes)).
- **First appears:** Book 4 (Jesus era)
- **Book 5 appearances:** chapter 1 (referenced as ancestor; his descendant [Quintus Publius Scipio](quintus_publius_scipio.md) is the on-page Gen 51 carrier), chapter 2 (genetic memory visions experienced by Augustine), chapter 5 (biography notes).
- **Raw sources:** `../../book_5_augustine_protocol/manuscript/chapters/chapter_01_constantine_era.md`, chapter_02_the_baptism.md, chapter_04_the_bishop.md

## Book 4 era (1st century CE)

- Roman census official, witnesses Jesus's crucifixion 32 CE as a young man
- Receives direct teaching from Jesus: "Pattern recognition. Systems cascade. Intervene early. Distribute knowledge. Never centralize."
- Hides with Miriam (Syrian, Gen 51 — **not** [Miriam of Magdala](miriam_of_magdala.md)) before Jerusalem burns in 70 CE
- Infiltrates offensive network in Alexandria (teaches defensive network how the offensive network thinks)
- Dies within a normal lifespan (~pre-100 CE)

## Book 5 era — his *line*, not him personally

Marcus Publius himself is dead by the time Book 5 opens (312 CE). His descendants carry the Pattern Eye forward. The key chain reconnects to on-page characters at his 4th-century descendant [Quintus Publius Scipio](quintus_publius_scipio.md), who receives Pattern Eye from [Helena](helena_mother_of_constantine.md) in Jerusalem in 327 CE and hands it to young [Monnica](monica.md) at Carthage in 352 CE. See [seven_keys.md](../seven_keys.md) for the full Pattern Eye chain.

## Continuity notes

- **Marcus Publius is confined to the 1st century CE** (resolved 2026-04-23). The 4th-c figure in Book 5 chapter 1 is his descendant [Quintus Publius Scipio](quintus_publius_scipio.md), named and written into the ch 1 rewrite on 2026-04-24.
- **Pattern Eye chain (canonical as of 2026-04-24 ch 1 rewrite):** Alexandria carrier (~307) → [Helena](helena_mother_of_constantine.md) (~312, Trier/Nicomedia) → [Quintus Publius Scipio](quintus_publius_scipio.md) (Jerusalem, 327 CE, at the Golgotha excavation) → [Monnica](monica.md) (Carthage, 352 CE, on Quintus's deathbed, Monnica is ~21) → [Augustine](augustine_of_hippo.md) (Ostia, 387 CE, on Monnica's deathbed) → [Possidius](possidius.md) (Hippo, 430 CE, at Augustine's deathbed) → [Atticus](atticus.md) of Constantinople (post-430).
- **No 410 CE Julia handoff.** The old ch 1 contained a second transfer scene at the sack of Rome; this was a custody contradiction with ch 2's 387 Ostia transfer. The rewrite deletes the Julia handoff entirely — Pattern Eye has been with Augustine in Hippo since 387 CE and is nowhere near Rome in 410.
- **Two Miriams problem:** Book 5 manuscript introduces a "Miriam, Gen 51, Syrian branch" hiding with Marcus before 70 CE. This is **NOT** [Miriam of Magdala](miriam_of_magdala.md) (who is Gen 42 per bloodline tracker). Naming overlap is significant — disambiguation still needed.
- **"17 generations from Marcus Publius" in the outline is wrong.** With Marcus Gen 51 and Augustine Gen 52 (canonical per 2026-04-23 resolution), Augustine is descended from Marcus via a longer side-branch than a direct 17-generation chain; the gen numbers indicate Marcus is an ancestor on one branch, Augustine's direct-line ancestors are on another. Outline needs a correction.
