# Miriam of Magdala (Miriam bat Ezra)

Defensive carrier, **Gen 42 (Absolute, Branch 3)** — same generation as [Jesus](jesus.md). Parallel protagonist of Book 4. Strongest carrier of her branch in the Roman era. Inherits Keys 2 and 3 after the crucifixion. Mother of [Sarah bat Miriam](sarah_bat_miriam.md).

- **Bloodline:** Branch 3 — **⚠️ raw is internally contradictory** (Tausret line per Phase 6 entry; Nefer's line per branch summary). See [bloodlines.md](../bloodlines.md).
- **Dates:** ~1 BCE – 70 CE (age ~71 at death)
- **Location:** Magdala → Capernaum (27 CE onwards) → diaspora after 70 CE
- **First appears:** Book 4 — Love Remembers in Silence
- **Status at end of latest canon:** Survives 33 CE, hides Keys 2 and 3 before 70 CE, teaches until death
- **Raw sources:** `../../SERIES_BIBLE_master_timeline.md`, `../../SERIES_BIBLE_bloodline_tracker.md` (lines 264–294)
