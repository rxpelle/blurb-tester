# Melania the Elder

Wealthy Roman matron; founder of the Mount of Olives monastery in Jerusalem; Gen 52 defensive carrier. Grandmother of [Melania the Younger](melania_the_younger.md). Establishes the Eastern preservation node that hides the **Memory Stone** (one of the seven keys per Book 5 manuscript taxonomy) in the baptistery foundation.

- **Bloodline:** Gen 52.
- **Dates:** ~350–410 CE (historical)
- **First appears:** Book 5 — referenced in chapters 7–8 (no on-page scenes; established as the founding-generation behind the Jerusalem network)
- **Raw sources:** `../../book_5_augustine_protocol/manuscript/chapters/chapter_07_fall_of_rome.md`, chapter_08_city_of_god.md

## Book 5 role

- **Founds Mount of Olives monastery** — the structural Eastern node of the defensive preservation network
- **Hides Memory Stone** in the baptistery foundation
- **Establishes the institutional template** her granddaughter [Melania the Younger](melania_the_younger.md) inherits and replicates across the Mediterranean

## Continuity notes

- Melania the Elder is offstage in Book 5's main timeline (she dies ~410 CE, around Augustine's mid-career). Her structural importance is generational: she sets up the Eastern monastery network before Rome falls, so when collapse arrives, the Eastern preservation infrastructure already exists.
- Naming overlap with Melania the Younger is historical, not invented; both are real figures.
