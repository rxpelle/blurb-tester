# Maria of Toledo

14-year-old Jewish physician's daughter in 1347 Toledo. **IS** [Defensive Key 1](../seven_keys.md#key-1-the-living-key) — the Living Key. **Gen 64 (Absolute)** — 22 generations from [Jesus](jesus.md), not 41 as the master timeline mistakenly claimed.

- **Bloodline:** Main Tausret line (Spanish Jewish branch)
- **Dates:** 1333–1405 CE (lives to 72)
- **Age in Book 1:** 14 (1347–1348)
- **Occupation:** Physician (trained by father)
- **First appears:** Book 1 — The Aethelred Cipher
- **Status at end of latest canon:** Goes underground; Rafael (youngest son) moved east via defensive network
- **Raw sources:** `../../SERIES_BIBLE_master_timeline.md`, `../../SERIES_BIBLE_bloodline_tracker.md` (lines 533–567)

## Book 1 — The Aethelred Cipher

- Daughter of a Jewish physician in Toledo (major Jewish intellectual center; Reconquista era)
- Carries defensive genetic markers — she *is* the Living Key
- "Flesh made cipher, memory in blood"
- **Blood memory** reveals network is "much older" than the medieval branch knows — accesses Bronze Age memories the Eltville network has forgotten
- Order hunts her during plague chaos; defensive network protects her
- Contemporary with [Thomas of Eltville](thomas_of_eltville.md) (both ~Gen 63–64) — they **never meet**, network keeps carriers separate for protection

## Children (three survive Black Death)

1. **Eldest son** — stays Spain, converso line → wiped out in Spanish Inquisition (1480s)
2. **Daughter** — marries French nobility → diluted within 3 generations
3. **Rafael (youngest son)** — defensive network moves him east, founding the Silk Road migration that leads to [Sarah Chen](sarah_chen.md) (Gen 65)

## Legacy

- Dies 1405 (lives 58 more years after Black Death)
- Rafael's line carries Key 3 (Memory Bridge) eastward over the next 4 generations to Guangzhou
- Sarah Chen = Gen 112, 48 generations after Maria, 671 years later
