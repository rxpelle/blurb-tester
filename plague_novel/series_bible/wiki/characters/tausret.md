# Tausret (Pharaoh Twosret)

Last pharaoh of Egypt's 19th Dynasty. Dies 1177 BCE — her death triggers the succession crisis that produces the Genesis Protocol. Founder of the defensive bloodline.

- **Bloodline:** Source of the defensive Living Key lineage
- **First appears:** Book 3 — The First Key
- **Status at end of latest canon:** Dies 1177 BCE (before Protocol creation completes)
- **Raw sources:** `../../SERIES_BIBLE_master_timeline.md`, `../../SERIES_BIBLE_bloodline_tracker.md`

## Book 3 — The First Key (1177 BCE)

Dies month 1. Palace purge targets her loyalists. Her daughter escapes to Canaan — seeding the bloodline that will run 111 generations to Sarah Chen. Her personal physician [Nefertari](nefertari.md) uses her death as catalyst for the defensive Protocol.
