# Thomas of Eltville

Monk, scribe, and **defensive carrier** of the medieval German branch. **Gen 63 (Absolute) / Gen 41 (Local from Adalbert).** Book 1 main protagonist. Doesn't know he's part of a 3,000-year-old defensive network — believes the network started with [Adalbert](adalbert_von_eltville.md) ~900 years ago.

- **Bloodline:** Branch 1 (medieval German line via Adalbert)
- **Dates:** 1320–1350 CE (dies age 30)
- **Age in Book 1:** 27 (1347 CE)
- **First appears:** Book 1 — The Aethelred Cipher
- **Status at end of latest canon:** Dies ~1350 CE (Order's hunt or Black Death aftermath)
- **Raw sources:** `../../SERIES_BIBLE_master_timeline.md`, `../../SERIES_BIBLE_bloodline_tracker.md` (lines 485–530)

## Genetic memory

**VERY STRONG** — manifests as exceptional pattern-recognition:
- Sees collapse patterns without understanding what they are
- Solves ciphers by *feeling* the pattern
- Spatial reasoning and systems thinking exceptional
- Dreams of places he's never been (unrecognized genetic memory surfacing)

## Book 1 — The Aethelred Cipher

- Trained by his grandfather [Wilhelm von Eltville](wilhelm_von_eltville.md), who passed him the **Iron Key** (raw is internally inconsistent on whether this is one of the seven bronze keys or a separate iron artifact — see [seven_keys.md](../seven_keys.md))
- Discovers the Order's offensive breeding records (the Order had suppressed all references to the defensive mode — Thomas only finds half the picture)
- Seeks other keys to understand the complete cipher system
- Copies offensive records and distributes to 11 cities before dying: Mainz, Strasbourg, Prague, Vienna, Paris, Avignon, Florence, Venice, Barcelona, London, Lübeck
- The 11 cities unknowingly align with ancient defensive network nodes — Thomas *accidentally* activates dormant defensive infrastructure
- **Blood memory visions** reveal Bronze Age origins — realizes he's part of something far older than Adalbert
- Dies ~1350 protecting the knowledge distribution

## Family / relationships

- Grandfather: [Wilhelm von Eltville](wilhelm_von_eltville.md) — taught him ciphers, passed Iron Key
- Father: Friedrich von Eltville (1261–1312) — died when Thomas was a child (raw doesn't elaborate)
- Partner: **Margarethe** (Heinrich's daughter, also has pattern abilities) — Book 1 secondary character
- Contemporary with [Maria of Toledo](maria_of_toledo.md): both ~Gen 63–64, but **never meet** (defensive network keeps carriers separate for protection)

## Legacy

- Becomes legend — but as the *incomplete hero* (only had half the picture)
- Knowledge fragments preserved through network
- His blood-memory discoveries hint at the Bronze Age origin to future readers/network members
- Thomas's medieval branch (Branch 1) **may have ended at his death** — line lost track after ~1350
- The Main Line continues via Maria → Rafael → Chen → [Sarah Chen](sarah_chen.md), 49 generations later in 2018
