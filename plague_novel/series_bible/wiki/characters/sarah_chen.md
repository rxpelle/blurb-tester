- **First appears:** Book 2 — The Genesis Protocol (2018 CE)
- **Status at end of latest canon:** Partially activates defensive Protocol; prevents THRESHOLD collapse

- Intellectual tradition selecting for pattern recognition over generations
- Genetic memory in stable non-coding DNA regions (not selected against)
