# Amenhotep

Court official under [Tausret](tausret.md). Rival of [Nefertari](nefertari.md). Creates the offensive Protocol. Founder of [the Order / offensive network](../factions/the_order.md).

- **Bloodline:** Offensive network progenitor (breeding-program origin)
- **First appears:** Book 3 — The First Key (1177 BCE)
- **Status at end of latest canon:** Alive and consolidating power in post-collapse Egypt (20th Dynasty); death date TBD
- **Raw sources:** `../../SERIES_BIBLE_master_timeline.md` (lines 34–55)

## Book 3 — The First Key

1177 BCE: Creates offensive Protocol as rival to Nefer's defensive system. Retains 3 bronze keys — Blood Register, Catastrophe Clock, Culling Method — which stay centralized with the offensive network through all 3,195 years to GenVault.

Stays in Egypt after the network split. Consolidates offensive power through 20th Dynasty (post-Tausret).

## Continuity notes

- Three offensive keys never disperse — they remain with the Order/GenVault across every era. This is a structural fixed point of the series.
- Raw doesn't give him a post-1177 BCE biography — defines his *legacy* not his *life*. Book 3 draft will need to flesh him out.
