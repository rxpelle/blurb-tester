# The Order (Offensive Network / GenVault)

Centralized, institutional, value-encoding faction. Founded 1177 BCE by [Amenhotep](../characters/amenhotep.md). 3,195 years of continuous operation — always hiding inside the dominant institution of its era.

- **First appears:** Book 3 — The First Key
- **Status at latest canon:** GenVault corporate form since 1952; THRESHOLD at 12.8% of global population engineered as of 2018 CE
- **Raw sources:** `../../SERIES_BIBLE_master_timeline.md`, `../../SERIES_BIBLE_network_evolution.md`

## Origin

**1177 BCE, Egypt:** Amenhotep creates offensive Protocol as rival to Nefer's defensive version. Retains 3 bronze keys — **Blood Register**, **Catastrophe Clock**, **Culling Method** — which stay with the Order across every era. The three offensive keys never disperse. This is a structural fixed point.

## Institutional shells across eras (always hides inside the dominant institution)

- **1177–900 BCE:** 20th Dynasty Egypt → Phoenician trade networks → Mediterranean city-states
- **586 BCE:** Infiltrates Jewish priesthood during Babylonian exile
- **500 BCE:** Temple Sadducees (priestly aristocracy)
- **26–33 CE (Book 4):** Cooperates with Rome against Jesus
- **70 CE:** Temple destroyed → pivots to infiltrating early Christianity
- **100–325 CE:** Controls bishop appointments in major cities; uses Church patronage for consolidation
- **325 CE:** Pushes hierarchical Church at Council of Nicaea
- **500–1200 CE:** Church hierarchy + nobility; arranged noble marriages = breeding program cover
- **1347 CE (Book 1):** Monastery network controlling knowledge; exposed (incompletely) by Thomas
- **1350–1500:** Transforms after exposure — diversifies into nobility, courts, merchant guilds; uses Inquisition to silence defensive carriers
- **1500–1700:** Splits Catholic/Protestant during Reformation; scientific societies become new base (Royal Society, 1660); "scientific breeding" replaces "divine bloodlines" language
- **1700–1900:** Universities, medical schools; Darwin (1859) provides cover for eugenics
- **1900–1950:** Nazi eugenics scandal → goes underground → embeds in pharmaceutical companies; Cold War bioweapons research provides cover
- **1952:** **GenVault founded** — 7 corporations, all Order-controlled. Modern face: disease prevention, genetic medicine. Actual purpose: offensive Protocol breeding.
- **2018 (Book 2):** THRESHOLD at 12.8%, targeting 15% = 1.4B planned deaths

## Book-by-book presence

### Book 1 — The Aethelred Cipher (1347)
Monastery network controls knowledge. Thomas of Eltville discovers breeding records and distributes them — Order exposed but survives by transforming.

### Book 2 — The Genesis Protocol (2018)
GenVault corporate form. Nearly killed Marsh (1982), buried Otto Fischer research (1995), flagged Sarah (2017). Defeated at THRESHOLD.

### Book 3 — The First Key (1177 BCE)
Amenhotep founds. Holds 3 offensive keys from day one. Consolidates post-Tausret.

### Book 4 — Love Remembers in Silence (26–70 CE)
Temple Sadducees. Sees Jesus's uncontrollable keyless access as existential threat. Cooperates with Rome in crucifixion. Temple destroyed 70 CE → pivots to Church infiltration.

### Book 5 — Augustine Protocol (312–476 CE)
Pushes hierarchical, centralized Church at Nicaea. Church hierarchy becomes the Order's political vehicle for the next 900 years.

## Continuity notes

- **Naming convention by era:** "Amenhotep's network" (Books 3, 4 early) → "Temple Sadducees" (Book 4 late) → "the Order" (Books 5–10) → "GenVault" (Book 2, modern). Use the contemporary name per era.
- **Three offensive keys stay together** — never disperse. Structural fixed point for the series.
- Value-encoding (ideological) — opposite of defensive network's empirical encoding. They don't just preserve — they *engineer*.
- Always inside the dominant institution of the era: Egyptian court → Jewish priesthood → Roman Church → universities → pharma. Pattern should be visible to readers across books.
