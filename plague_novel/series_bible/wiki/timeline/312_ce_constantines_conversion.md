- **Date (in-world):** 312 CE (Battle of Milvian Bridge) through 476 CE (Western Rome falls)

## What happens

[Constantine](../characters/constantine.md)'s vision at Milvian Bridge combines genuine spiritual/political experience with low-grade genetic memory activation inherited through his mother **Helena** (Gen 50, weak carrier — needs wiki page during proper manuscript ingest). The chi-rho is read by the defensive network as Protocol-adjacent symbolism. Constantine himself doesn't fully decode what he's experiencing.

**325 CE — Council of Nicaea:** Offensive network pushes hierarchical, centralized Church (control). Defensive network pushes contemplative theology (preservation). Compromise: both survive in orthodox Christianity. Keys split — 2 defensive in monasteries (encoded in contemplative practice), 3 offensive in hierarchy.

**354–430 CE — [Augustine of Hippo](../characters/augustine_of_hippo.md):** Defensive carrier, Gen 52, descended from Marcus Publius (Book 4 → Book 5 bridge). His 386 CE conversion is *both* genuine spiritual conversion *and* genetic memory activation triggered by his mother **Monica** (network operative — needs wiki page during ingest). *Confessions* and *City of God* are deliberately encoded defensive Protocol manuals hidden inside orthodox theology.

**410 CE:** Visigoths sack Rome. Pagans blame Christianity; Christians in crisis. Offensive network's "control prevents collapse" narrative breaks. Augustine begins *City of God* in response.

**430 CE:** Augustine dies during Vandal siege of Hippo. **Melania** evacuates manuscripts and bronze keys to monasteries before city falls.

**476 CE:** Western Empire officially falls. Dark Ages begin. Monasteries = defensive vehicle (preservation). Church hierarchy = offensive vehicle (control). Both modes survive through Christianity — carried by institutions *and* by genetic descent through carriers like Augustine's lineage.
