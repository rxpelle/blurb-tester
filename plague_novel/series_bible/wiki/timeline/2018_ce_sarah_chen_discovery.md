## What happens

[Sarah Chen](../characters/sarah_chen.md), 28, GenVault genetic epidemiologist, discovers she IS the Living Key ([Defensive Key 1](../seven_keys.md)) — 82nd generation from Tausret, 28 from Maria of Toledo. First defensive activation attempt in 670 years.

Current offensive state: **12.8% of global population engineered**. THRESHOLD target: **15%**. Planned deaths at THRESHOLD: **1.4 billion** (population reduction event).

**Sarah's quest:** Find Keys 2–4 to activate defensive mode before THRESHOLD triggers.
- **Key 2 (Pattern Eye):** St. Ansgar monastery ruins, White Mountains, New Hampshire (arrived 1890s via Moravian Brethren → Pennsylvania → NH)
- **Key 3 (Memory Bridge):** Chen family archive, Guangzhou (arrived ~1465 via Rafael's line)
- **Key 4 (Distribution Network):** Encoded in Thomas's 11 cities (1347)

**Backstory threads:** 1982 — Marsh attempts exposure, nearly killed. 1995 — Otto Fischer research buried. 2015 — David Chen begins GenVault hacking. 2017 — Sarah flagged in GenVault genetic screening.

**Outcome:** Sarah gathers 3 defensive keys, partially activates defensive Protocol, prevents THRESHOLD collapse. David Chen uploads defensive mode public. 3,000-year conflict reaches turning point.
