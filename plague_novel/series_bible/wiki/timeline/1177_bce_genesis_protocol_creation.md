# 1177 BCE — Genesis Protocol Creation

- **Date (in-world):** 1177 BCE (Bronze Age Collapse year)
- **Book(s):** Book 3 — The First Key
- **Chapter(s):** Opening arc
- **Location:** Pi-Ramesses, Egypt (Nile Delta)
- **Raw sources:** `../../SERIES_BIBLE_master_timeline.md` (lines 21–73)

## What happens

Pharaoh Tausret (last of 19th Dynasty) dies month 1. Succession crisis ignites palace purge. Over months 2–6, [Nefertari](../characters/nefertari.md) creates the genetic memory encoding system (defensive Protocol) while rival [Amenhotep](../characters/amenhotep.md) builds an offensive counterpart. Seven bronze keys forged by Nefertari. Months 7–12: network ruptures. Defensive network flees Egypt into Mediterranean diaspora; offensive network consolidates in Egypt (20th Dynasty under Setnakhte). Bronze keys split 4 defensive / 3 offensive. Tausret's daughter escapes to Canaan — founds the Living Key bloodline.

## Who is present

- [Tausret](../characters/tausret.md) (dies month 1)
- [Nefertari](../characters/nefertari.md) — defensive Protocol creator
- [Amenhotep](../characters/amenhotep.md) — offensive Protocol creator
- Bay — chancellor, manipulating succession
- Setnakhte — seizes throne, founds 20th Dynasty

## What changes as a result

- 3,000-year conflict begins between the two networks
- [Seven Keys](../seven_keys.md) split and enter recorded distribution
- Tausret's bloodline (defensive) begins — later becomes Jesus (41 gen), Maria of Toledo (83 gen), Sarah Chen (111 gen)
- Offensive network controls Egypt for next ~300 years
- Historical context: Bronze Age Collapse (90% population decline, Mycenaean/Hittite/Ugarit/Troy falling)
