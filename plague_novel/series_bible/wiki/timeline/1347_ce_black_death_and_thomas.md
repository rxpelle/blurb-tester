# 1347 CE — Black Death & Thomas's Distribution

- **Date (in-world):** 1347–1348 CE
- **Book(s):** Book 1 — The Aethelred Cipher
- **Location:** Central/Western Europe — 11 cities: Mainz, Strasbourg, Prague, Vienna, Paris, Avignon, Florence, Venice, Barcelona, London, Lübeck
- **Raw sources:** `../../SERIES_BIBLE_master_timeline.md` (lines 385–431)

## What happens

Bubonic plague begins (30–60% European population dies). Thomas of Eltville, monk and scribe, discovers the [Order's](../factions/the_order.md) offensive breeding records — but the Order has suppressed all references to the defensive mode, so he finds only half the picture.

[Maria of Toledo](../characters/maria_of_toledo.md), 14-year-old daughter of a Jewish physician, is the Living Key ([Defensive Key 1](../seven_keys.md#key-1-the-living-key)) — 41st generation from Jesus. Parallels Jesus's own 41st-generation marker from Tausret.

**Thomas's distribution:** Copies offensive breeding records, distributes to 11 cities before dying of plague. Unknowingly the 11 cities align with ancient defensive network nodes — he *accidentally* activates dormant defensive infrastructure.

**Maria's fate:** Order hunts her. Defensive network scatters her children. Rafael (youngest son) moved Venice → Constantinople → Samarkand → Guangzhou by 1465. Maria's other children killed (Inquisition) or diluted.

## Who is present

- [Thomas of Eltville](../characters/thomas_of_eltville.md) — monk, unknowing distributor
- [Maria of Toledo](../characters/maria_of_toledo.md) — Living Key, Book 1 protagonist
- Rafael — Maria's youngest son, carries bloodline east

## What changes as a result

- Order exposed (incompletely) but survives by transforming — diversifies into nobility, courts, merchant guilds
- Defensive keys remain hidden
- Thomas becomes legend (incomplete hero — only had half the picture)
- Maria's bloodline goes underground; travels east via Silk Road
- Inquisition becomes Order's tool to silence defensive carriers (1350–1500)
