## Canon vs planned

Canon = what ships in a published book. Currently canon through Book 3. Book 4 is in late edits — treat it as canon-pending; lint conflicts against 1–3 are hard errors, conflicts against 4 are soft warnings until launch.

**Book 5 has a complete first-draft manuscript** (9 chapters, 45,411 words, Jan 2026 — `book_5_augustine_protocol/manuscript/`). Treat as **draft-canon**: stronger than "planned" but not yet through edits. Lint conflicts against the Book 5 manuscript are real conflicts to surface, but should be flagged for editorial decision rather than auto-resolved against earlier "planned" notes.

Books 6+ are planned only. **Important process lesson (2026-04-22):** before treating any later book as "planned only," check the book folder for a manuscript first. Book 5 was assumed unwritten and got mistakenly canonized against its own manuscript before the manuscript was discovered.

## Scope note (flagged 2026-04-22 during first ingest)

The legacy `SERIES_BIBLE_master_timeline.md` describes a **5-book series titled "Genesis Protocol"**. The current plan is **14 books under "The Architecture of Survival"**. The raw doc predates the rename/expansion and does not cover Books 6–12 or Book 13 (Synthesis Protocol).

**⚠️ The earlier "41st-generation pattern" hypothesis was a master-timeline artifact (now flagged as wrong).** The real progression is **Gen 1 → 42 → 64 → 112** — no repeating 41-gen spans. Drop the pattern hypothesis.

Two parallel counting systems exist in-world: **Absolute** (used by this wiki, Books 2–5+) and **Local** (used by Book 1's medieval network, counting from [Adalbert von Eltville](characters/adalbert_von_eltville.md) ~400 CE). [Thomas of Eltville](characters/thomas_of_eltville.md) is Gen 41 Local = Gen 63 Absolute. See [bloodlines.md](bloodlines.md).
