## THEMATIC ELEMENTS TO EMPHASIZE

### Dual-Layer Teaching Methodology
- Every parable has surface (spiritual) and embedded (systems) meaning
- Most people hear surface, carriers hear both
- Encoding for cultural transmission across diaspora
- Pattern recognition requires training, not just hearing

### Knowledge Distribution Strategy
- Teaching multiplication: Each trained person teaches others
- Mary's women's circle = parallel network (resilience through redundancy)
- Not centralized (Jesus-dependent) but distributed (network-dependent)
- Success measured by how many can teach, not how many follow

### Escalation Dynamics
- Rome/Temple/Zealots all locked in mutual escalation
- Each faction's response provokes the others
- Intervention points passed (can't prevent, only prepare)
- Jesus teaching de-escalation while system accelerates toward violence

### Martyrdom as Encoding
- Death isn't failure—it's part of the strategy
- Trauma encodes memory more deeply (blood memory mechanism)
- Persecution validates teaching (proves power structures threatened)
- Resurrection narrative = teaching continuity (symbolic, not literal)

### Time Pressure
- Timeline compressing (2 years → 18 months → 12 months)
- Never enough time to finish preparation
- Encode essentials now, trust distribution later
- Imperfect transmission better than perfect concealment
