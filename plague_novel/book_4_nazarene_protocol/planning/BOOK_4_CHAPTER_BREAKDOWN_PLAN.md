## TONE & STYLE GUIDELINES

### Voice:
- Clinical precision mixed with emotional depth
- Historical grounding with speculative theology
- Systems thinking language embedded naturally
- Biblical cadence where appropriate, modern clarity elsewhere

### POV Strategy:
- Multiple POVs (Marcus, Mary, Jesus, Simon, Judas, Helena)
- Each POV offers different network perspective
- Jesus POV: Internal, philosophical, systems-focused
- Mary POV: Emotional, transformative, pattern-recognition learning
- Marcus POV: External, political, moral conflict
- Simon POV: Network elder, practical, preservationist

### Blood Memory Depiction:
- Dreams/visions are vivid but fragmentary
- Sensory detail (smell, sound, emotional texture)
- Not prophetic, but pattern-recognition of past
- Strengthens each generation (Jesus sees age 3, Mary age 7)
- Can be overwhelming, intrusive, destabilizing

### Theological Reframing Rules:
