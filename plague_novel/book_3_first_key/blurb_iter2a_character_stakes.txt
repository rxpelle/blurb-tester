Egyptian physician Nefertari has discovered something that will change the world forever — or destroy it.

In the dying Bronze Age, a pattern hidden in plague and death reveals humanity's darkest secret. When Nefertari and her lover Amenhotep forge seven bronze keys to protect this knowledge, they tear mankind in two.

One faction will kill to keep the secret buried. The other will slaughter millions to control it.

For eight centuries, their descendants wage shadow war across the ancient world. From Egypt's burning libraries to Alexander's deathbed, blood flows in service of a truth too dangerous to speak.

But the keys are surfacing. The war is escalating.

And Nefertari's choice has damned everyone she loves.

Some knowledge should stay buried. Some wars never end.
