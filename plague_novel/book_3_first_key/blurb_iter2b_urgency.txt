The Bronze Age dies in blood. The secret war begins now.

Egyptian physician Nefertari sees death's pattern. A code hidden in plague. Knowledge that could remake or destroy humanity.

She forges seven bronze keys. Splits mankind in two.

For nine centuries, shadow armies clash. One side prevents the next collapse. The other plots to control it. Real history becomes battleground as descendants hunt the keys across Alexander's empire.

The keys surface. The war escalates. The final battle approaches.

But Nefertari's greatest enemy isn't the faction hunting her — it's the choice she made to save the man she loves.

Some secrets birth wars. Some wars last forever.
