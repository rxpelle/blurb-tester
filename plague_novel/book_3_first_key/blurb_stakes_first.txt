The Bronze Age is ending in blood and ash, but the real war is just beginning.

When Egyptian physician Nefertari witnesses a pattern hidden in plague and death — a biological code that could reshape human destiny — she and her lover Amenhotep make a choice that will echo for nine centuries. They forge seven bronze keys to preserve this dangerous knowledge, unknowingly splitting mankind into two secret armies.

One side fights to prevent the next collapse. The other plots to control it.

From the burning libraries of Egypt to Alexander's final breath, carriers of an inherited genetic memory guard the most explosive secret in human history. But with each generation, their enemies grow stronger, evolving from scattered observers into the ruthless organization that will one day call itself the Order.

Eight hundred forty-two years. Seven bronze keys. Two factions locked in shadow war.

In The First Key, real history collides with a conspiracy that shaped the ancient world — and the men who would rule it.

Some knowledge is too dangerous to exist. Too powerful to destroy.
