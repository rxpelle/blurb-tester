What if humanity's greatest secret was hidden in plain sight for three thousand years?

Egyptian physician Nefertari discovers a pattern in plague and death — something that splits mankind into two warring factions. She forges seven bronze keys to protect this knowledge, but her choice dooms everyone she loves.

Nine centuries of shadow war follow. Real battles. Real blood. Real history shaped by a conspiracy spanning Alexander's conquests to Egypt's fall.

One faction fights to prevent humanity's collapse. The other seeks to control it.

But as the bronze keys surface and ancient enemies close in, Nefertari faces an impossible truth: the greatest threat isn't what she discovered.

It's what she became to protect it.

Some choices echo through eternity. Some wars are written in our blood.
