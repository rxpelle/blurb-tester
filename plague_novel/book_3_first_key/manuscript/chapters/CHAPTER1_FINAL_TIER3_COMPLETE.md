## ENHANCEMENT #2: DEEPENED AMENHOTEP CONFESSION (+280 words) ✅

**Location:** Lines 268-291 (confession/split scene)

**Original:** Simple dialogue exchange ending relationship
**Revised:** Physical intimacy moment showing what they're losing

**New Content Added:**
```
He crossed the workshop. Stopped a hand's width away.

She could feel the heat coming off him. Bronze dust on his skin. The smell of beeswax and sweat. Twenty years of working side by side and they'd never been this close. Never let themselves.

"Nefertari." His voice was rough. "If you asked me to choose differently—"

"I won't." But her hand moved anyway. Found his. Their fingers threaded together like they'd done it a thousand times before instead of never.

His pulse against hers. Both racing. Both knowing this was the only time. The only moment before the split made them enemies.

"I know." He lifted their joined hands. Pressed his forehead to her knuckles. "I know you won't. That's why I—"

Love you. The words hung there. Unspoken.

She pulled her hand back. Had to. Because if she didn't pull back now she'd cross the gap between prevention and control, between saving everyone and choosing who lives. She'd compromise everything Tausret had asked her to preserve.
```

**Plus closing reflection:**
> It meant twenty years of might-have-been turning into three thousand years of opposition. It meant choosing principles over the only person who'd ever understood her completely. It meant the workshop would be empty tomorrow and she'd still smell beeswax and remember how his pulse had felt against her palm.

**Impact:**
- Shows exactly what they're sacrificing (not just telling)
- Physical intimacy (hand-holding, forehead press) is period-appropriate
- Pulse-against-pulse imagery ties to their work as physicians
- "Love you" unspoken = devastating restraint
- Makes the split feel like tragic inevitability, not just philosophical disagreement
- Reader feels the loss viscerally
