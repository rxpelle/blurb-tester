## Writing Style Guidelines

### Tone & Style
- Publication-ready literary thriller prose
- Rich sensory details (smells, textures, sounds, visual specifics)
- Deep emotional interiority for POV characters
- No emojis or casual language
- Grounded, visceral action sequences
- Smart dialogue that reveals character

### Medieval Timeline Specifics
- Sensory details: Cold, medieval smells, physical exhaustion, period-appropriate settings
- Thomas's voice: Scholarly, questioning genetic determinism
- Margarethe's voice: Tactical, fierce, protective
- Maria's voice: Ancient wisdom processing her design
- Period-appropriate language without archaic "thee/thou"

### Modern Timeline Specifics
- Sensory details: Institutional spaces, technology, urban/facility environments
- Sarah's voice: Scientific precision, defiant despite captivity
- Clinical language contrasted with human cost
- Contemporary feel without dating the story
