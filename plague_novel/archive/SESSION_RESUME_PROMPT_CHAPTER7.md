## Story Summary

### The Conspiracy
A 900-year-old network founded during Rome's collapse to preserve knowledge through dark ages has corrupted its mission. Originally defensive (help humanity survive collapse), it became offensive (engineer collapse to benefit chosen bloodlines). Modern corporations (GenVault + 6 others) plan to release THRESHOLD - a genetic targeting virus that kills anyone without protective markers, eliminating 85-90% of humanity.

### Medieval Timeline (1347)
Thomas, Margarethe, and Maria are gathering seven keys that unlock the complete Genesis Protocol. Each key reveals a fragment:
1. Iron Key - locations
2. Silver Key - connections
3. Maria - genetic memory interpretation
4. Voice that Speaks - temporal patterns (OBTAINED)
5. Foot that Walks - movement patterns (Constantinople)
6. Heart that Feels - emotional/cultural context (England)
7. Mind that Knows - complete synthesis (Constantinople)

### Modern Timeline (Present)
Dr. Sarah Chen infiltrated GenVault, photographed THRESHOLD evidence, was captured. The original network (Martin, Elena, Marcus, etc.) distributed her evidence globally. Otto Aris provided historical Protocol documentation proving the network betrayed its founding purpose. Wells is deploying THRESHOLD in 72 hours despite being at 14.9% coverage instead of 15%.

## Writing Style Guidelines

### Tone & Style
- Publication-ready literary thriller prose
- Rich sensory details (smells, textures, sounds, visual specifics)
- Deep emotional interiority for POV characters
- No emojis or casual language
- Grounded, visceral action sequences
- Smart dialogue that reveals character

### Medieval Timeline Specifics
- Sensory details: Cold, frost, medieval smells (tanning, smoke, waste), physical exhaustion
- Thomas's voice: Scholarly, analytical, processing genetic determinism and free will
- Margarethe's voice: Tactical, blunt, fierce but reflective
- Maria's voice: Ancient wisdom in 14-year-old body, processing her designed existence
- Period-appropriate language without archaic "thee/thou"

### Modern Timeline Specifics
- Sensory details: Institutional white spaces, chemical smells, technology, urban environments
- Sarah's voice: Scientific precision, pattern recognition, defiant despite drugs
- Martin's voice: Tactical coordinator, grief over inability to rescue Sarah
- Wells's voice: Clinical detachment, philosophical justification of genocide
- Contemporary feel without dating the story
