**Option C: Hybrid approach**

**RECOMMENDATION: Option C - Show first child born and healthy**
- Makes timeline work (18 months = plenty of time for birth + recovery + new pregnancy)
- Avoids unexplained tragedy
- Shows life continuing/building
- More hopeful tone matching book's themes
