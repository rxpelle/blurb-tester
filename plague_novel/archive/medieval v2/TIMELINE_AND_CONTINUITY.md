### Issue #1: Johannes's Fate ✅ RESOLVED - NO CONTRADICTION

**What Actually Happened:**
Chapter 5 properly continues from Chapter 4. The sequence is:

1. **Chapter 4 ending**: Johannes captured at safe house in Strasbourg
2. **Chapter 5**: Thomas and Margarethe decide to rescue him despite the danger
3. They infiltrate the cathedral prison where he's being held
4. Successfully rescue him but he's mortally wounded from torture
5. He dies after giving them information about Maria
6. They go to St. Catherine's Convent to find Maria

**This is NOT a continuity error** - it's the intended plot. Chapter 5 is literally titled "The Rescue" and follows through on rescuing Johannes from captivity.

- Chapter 4 ends with them fleeing to abandoned cottage 5 miles outside Strasbourg
- Chapter 5 opens "Two Days After the Heist" in a "shepherd's hut"
- No explanation of how they learned Johannes was in cathedral prison (not dead)
- No explanation of why they're still so close to Strasbourg after 2 days

**RECOMMENDED FIX:**
Add brief transitional scene at END of Chapter 4:
- They receive message/rumor that Johannes is alive, held in cathedral
- This gives them the information they need for Chapter 5's rescue mission
- Explains why they haven't fled east yet
