**1. [Developmental Editor] Narrative Drive / Thriller Stakes** (All three sections — bronze box sequences)
- **Issue:** The thriller's central mystery object (the bronze disc) is invoked three times but its stakes are never made legible to the reader. We know the disc can be warm or cold, that Monica gave it to Augustine at Ostia in 387, and that it is 'for' a man who has not arrived.
