# The Aethelred Cipher - Book Description

## Amazon/Retailer Description (Under 4,000 characters)

**What if the Black Death wasn't an accident?**

1347. Brother Thomas thought his greatest challenge would be copying manuscripts in peace. Then he found his mentor murdered over a cipher that shouldn't exist—one that reveals locations across Europe where forbidden knowledge has been hidden for a thousand years.

The killer? A network that's been engineering humanity's greatest catastrophes since the fall of Rome.

Now Thomas is running for his life with two unlikely allies: Margarethe, a merchant's daughter who watched the network murder her father, and Maria, a fourteen-year-old girl carrying forty generations of forbidden memories encoded in her blood.

Together, they're racing across plague-ravaged Europe to unlock seven keys before the network recaptures them. Each key reveals another layer of a conspiracy so vast it makes the Black Death look like a side project.

Because the network doesn't just preserve knowledge through dark ages—they *create* the dark ages. Collapse isn't inevitable. It's profitable.

**But knowing the truth and surviving it are two different things.**

---

*Perfect for fans of Umberto Eco's THE NAME OF THE ROSE and Ken Follett's PILLARS OF THE EARTH.*

**The Aethelred Cipher** is the first book in The Genesis Protocol series, where medieval secrets and modern consequences collide. Each book stands alone, but together they tell the story of humanity's oldest conspiracy—and the small rebellions that might finally break it.
