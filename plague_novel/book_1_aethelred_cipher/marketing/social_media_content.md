## RECURRING CONTENT FORMATS

Use these as templates for ongoing posts beyond the 6-week calendar:

### "Things I Googled" Series
Share real search queries from the writing process. Always funny, always revealing.

### "Real History" Series
One historical fact per post that connects to the novel's themes.

### Quote Graphics
Book quotes on dark, textured backgrounds (manuscript, stone, candlelight aesthetic).

### "Character File" Series
Brief character introductions with personality traits, skills, and one defining quote.

### "The Pattern" Series
Modern events that echo the collapse patterns discussed in the blog posts. (Handle carefully — observe, don't preach.)

### Research Deep-Dives
Longer posts or threads explaining the real history behind specific scenes.
