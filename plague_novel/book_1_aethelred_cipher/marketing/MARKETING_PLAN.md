- [ ] Verify KDP formatting using existing Pandoc workflow (`publishing/KDP_FORMATTING_WORKFLOW.md`)

  - Headers/footers with page numbers
  - Chapter starts on right-side pages

- [ ] Create print-ready interior PDF

## Key Insight: Your Competitive Advantages

1. **The website is already live** with Google Analytics and ConvertKit - most debut authors don't have this
2. **The manuscript exists in multiple formats** (.md, .docx, .pdf) - you're ahead on production
3. **Cover art already exists** - decide if it meets professional standards or needs upgrading
4. **Series infrastructure is built** - randypellegrini.com already has Books, Series, Characters pages
5. **The Amazon description is strong** - "What if the Black Death wasn't an accident?" is a great hook
6. **12-book series plan** - gives you long-term marketing leverage (each new book sells old ones)

## Biggest Risks to Manage

1. **Cover quality** - The #1 factor in reader click-through. If current covers don't match pro genre standards, invest in a professional print wrap ($700-1,000). This is the highest-ROI spend.
2. **Reviews at launch** - Books with <20 reviews struggle on Amazon. ARC team recruitment is critical.
3. **Print formatting** - Proof copies are non-negotiable. What looks good on screen often has margin/alignment issues in print.
4. **Burnout** - Marketing takes 10-15 hours/week ongoing. Pick 2 platforms max and be consistent rather than trying to be everywhere.
