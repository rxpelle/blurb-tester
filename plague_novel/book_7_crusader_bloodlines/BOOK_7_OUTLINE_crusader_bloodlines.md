## ELEVATOR PITCH

A French healer carrying defensive network bloodlines joins the First Crusade and discovers both networks are using holy war as cover for the largest genetic experiment in history—mixing European, Byzantine, and Middle Eastern bloodlines while testing plague resistance at scale. As she races to find the Bronze Hand key hidden in Jerusalem, she must choose between exposing the conspiracy or completing her mission to unite scattered defensive networks across three continents.

## HISTORICAL CONTEXT (1095-1099 CE)

### The First Crusade
- **1095 CE:** Pope Urban II calls for crusade at Clermont
- **1096 CE:** Multiple crusader armies depart Europe (spring-summer)
- **1097 CE:** Siege of Nicaea, Battle of Dorylaeum
- **1098 CE:** Siege of Antioch (9 months), discovery of Holy Lance
- **1099 CE:** Siege of Jerusalem (June-July), city falls July 15

### Population Mixing Opportunity
- ~100,000 crusaders from Western Europe
- Byzantine Greeks (guides, allies, rivals)
- Arab, Persian, Turkish populations in path
- Jewish communities across route
- Massive mortality (~80% died from disease/battle)
- Unprecedented genetic mixing through rape, slavery, marriage

### Network Opportunities
- **Offensive Network:** Test disease resistance at scale, breed "optimal" survivors
- **Defensive Network:** Unite scattered Mediterranean networks, recover lost keys
- **Both:** Religious fervor provides perfect cover for eugenic goals

   - Brother Petros has access
   - Lost when Constantinople is sacked (1204 CE, after Book 7)

4. **Lead Shield** (Protection encoding)
   - Location 1095: Damascus, Arab defensive network
   - Not accessed in Book 7 (too dangerous to reach)
   - Mentioned but off-stage

   - Location 1095: Córdoba, Muslim Spain
   - Offensive network captured it (~1000 CE)
   - Used for coordinating crusade operations

6. **Silver Key** (Hand that Guides - DIFFERENT from Bronze Hand)
   - Location 1095: Unknown (will eventually reach Strasbourg by 1347)
   - Not found in Book 7 (Thomas steals it from Strasbourg Bishop in Book 1)

7. **Living Key** (Genetic carrier)
   - Book 7: Alienor herself (Gen 55)
   - Doesn't know she IS a key until revelation

**Book 7 Key Mission:**
- Alienor must find Bronze Hand before offensive network does
- Prevent them from using it to decode Jerusalem archives
- Unite defensive networks using key as proof of authority
