# Chapter 8-10 Bridge Summary

## Chapter 8 Ending (Updated)
**Timeline:** 6 hours before THRESHOLD deployment

**Key Elements:**
- 41 hubs secured out of 47 (87% interdiction rate)
- 6 hubs still operational for deployment
- Sarah's cooperation reduced projected casualties from 600M to 400M
- Morrison reveals this is "Phase One field validation" - a test run
- Phase Two planned for 5-7 years later with 3-4 billion casualties
- Morrison announces breeding program begins today, will introduce genetic partners
- Chapter ends with Sarah waiting for partner introductions in final hours

**Final line:** "And in six hours, THRESHOLD would deploy at six hubs simultaneously, beginning a Phase One field validation that would kill 400 million people while gathering data for Phase Two's real genocide. Sarah closed her eyes and waited for Morrison to return with her genetic partners—three men selected for compatibility, all as trapped as she was, all about to be introduced in the final hours before the world changed forever."
