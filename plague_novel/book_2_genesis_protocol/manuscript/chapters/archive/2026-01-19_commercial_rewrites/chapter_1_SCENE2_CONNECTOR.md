## Scene 2: The Escape Continues

Sarah circled back toward GenVault, keeping to side streets. She needed to know if Aris had escaped. If he was safe.

If he was still alive.

Headlights swept across her. A car screeched to a stop. Diesel exhaust, acrid in her throat.

Sarah's foot hit the brake—

The passenger window rolled down. Dr. Otto Aris looked out, his normally composed face haggard. He'd aged ten years since this afternoon.

"Get in. Leave your car. Now."

Sarah didn't hesitate. She grabbed her laptop bag, left the Civic running, and threw herself into the passenger seat. The interior smelled like old coffee and stale cigarette smoke—Aris had quit five years ago, but the car remembered.

Aris accelerated before she'd closed the door.
