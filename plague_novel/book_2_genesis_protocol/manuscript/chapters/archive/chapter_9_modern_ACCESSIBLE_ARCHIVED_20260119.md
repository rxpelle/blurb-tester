The death toll updated one final time: **1,387,491,203**

And counting.

Found nothing except grief and David's words: *Small rebellion. But rebellion nonetheless.*

She'd fought the network with everything she had.

Done everything one person could do against two-thousand-year conspiracy.

Enough to preserve evidence for future resistance.

Enough to matter, even if not enough to win.

Tomorrow she'd begin breeding for the network.

## PART TWO: THE ENDEMIC PHASE (Months 2-4)
