## KEY THEMES

1. **Prevention vs. Optimization**
   - Defensive Protocol (prevent collapse) vs. Offensive Protocol (engineer survivors)
   - Sarah proves prevention works imperfectly but works

2. **Complete vs. Partial Success**
   - 98% success (46/47 hubs) = 2% failure
   - Hub 47 escaped, causing global pandemic
   - 35M dead from the one hub that got away

3. **Cost of Incomplete Prevention**
   - Li Mei and family: the human face of 35M deaths
   - GenVault profits $189B from pandemic they created
   - Morrison's suspicious death ("convenient timing, unprovable murder")
   - Wells escapes with Archive, still at large

4. **Institutional Power Surviving**
   - GenVault survives: individuals blamed, corporation endures
   - Develops vaccine using THRESHOLD research to profit
   - Morrison takes sole blame as "rogue geneticist"

5. **Carrying Truth Alone — and Fighting Anyway**
   - Sarah can't tell students the truth (NDAs, Espionage Act)
   - Dead man's switch ensures truth survives even if she doesn't
   - Anonymous papers save thousands by laundering intelligence through mathematics
   - Tracking Wells's next deployment through counter-network
   - "Always yes. Even knowing. Always yes."
