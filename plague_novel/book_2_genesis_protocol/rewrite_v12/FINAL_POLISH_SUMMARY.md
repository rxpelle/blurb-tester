3. **Morrison's steady hands** - Control vs internal chaos
4. **Numbers as rhythm** - "47.3 million, 67.2 million, 89.4 million dead dead dead"
5. **Countdown timers** - Multiple timelines converging

### Stephen King Voice Elements:
- **Parentheticals:** ~180 total across all chapters (intimate narrator)
- **Fragments during stress:** Enhanced in all action sequences
- **Physical details over emotions:** Body betrayal, specific sensations
- **Dark humor:** Maintained throughout ("GenVault coffee is toxic. But not lethally.")
- **Specific numbers:** Names, ages, exact times make horror concrete

### Ethical Complexity Preserved:
- Morrison genuinely believes he's right
- No cartoon villainy
- Trolley problems have no easy answers
- Characters make hard choices for principled reasons
- "Was it worth it?" "Yes. Always yes."
