### B. Build Your "Code AGI Platform" Vision

**Create a strategic document that addresses:**

#### 1. **Infrastructure for Long-Horizon Agents**
What needs to change when developers (and non-developers) run agents that work for hours/days?

- **Compute/cost model:** Usage will shift from "few times daily" to "all day, multiple instances in parallel"
- **Security boundaries:** Agents making API calls, accessing databases, deploying code
- **Observability:** How do you monitor/debug agent behavior?
- **Rollback/safety:** "Trust and reliability" requirements from Dan Shipper's framework

#### 2. **Developer Experience for Agent Management**
The podcast says users will go from "working as an IC to managing a team of agents." Your DX role becomes:

- How do devs **orchestrate** multiple agents?
- How do they **validate** agent output?
- How do they **learn** what agents can do?
- What does **version control** look like for agent-generated code?

#### 3. **Democratization Strategy**
"Complete collapse between idea and execution for everyone" - not just developers

- Who else in the org should have access to capability creation?
- What guardrails/templates do they need?
- How do you prevent "AI slop and technical debt" (podcast sponsor's concern)?

**Deliverable:** A 2-3 page vision doc you can shop around internally

## VIII. Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: "Too visionary, not grounded enough"
**Mitigation:** Always pair vision with concrete pilots. Lead with data from your experiments.

### Risk 2: "Stepping on toes" (AI Strategy might be someone else's domain)
**Mitigation:** Frame as platform/infrastructure enablement, not AI strategy ownership. Partner, don't compete.

### Risk 3: "Moving too fast, organization isn't ready"
**Mitigation:** Run pilots quietly first. Build proof points before going loud.

### Risk 4: "Economic downturn or budget cuts"
**Mitigation:** Frame as efficiency play - Code AGI means "do more with same/less." This is a cost story, not just innovation.

### Risk 5: "Technology doesn't mature as fast as expected"
**Mitigation:** Even if timeline is off by 12 months, trajectory is clear. Your positioning still works. Just adjust pacing.
